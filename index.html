Rebus Rumble

Flip the card. Crack the wordplay. Shout it before the other team does.

Free · No ads · No accounts · A room is required to play

How to Play

  1. Host a Game — name your two teams and pick an optional Target Score. Tap Start Room to get a 4-letter room code.
  2. Join as Display — on the TV or laptop everyone can see, open this same page and enter the Host's room code.
  3. Start Game — the Host taps Start once the Display is connected.
  4. Each card is a classic picture rebus — word position, size, repetition, or a number standing in for its sound — that spells out one word or phrase.
  5. Shout it out — no typing, no buzzers. Teams just call out their guess as soon as they think they know it.
  6. The Host taps Team A got it / Team B got it to award the point and deal the next card, or Skip if nobody can get it.
  7. The game ends when the card deck runs out, or a team hits the target score — highest score wins (a tie is a draw).

A working room is required to play: the Host screen shows the answer, so it can't double as the shared Display.
